This form searches for crosspoint of two rays in spherical coordinate system.

Please put geolocations of two initial points and their rays azimuthes.
If initial point is located exactly on one of poles then its azimuth is assigned as desired longitude direction. The actual longitude does not matter in the case.
Generally there are two ortodrome crosspoints in the sphere, but the one with the shortest combined travel distance from both initial points is chosen.
In case if the ortodrome crosspoints are equally spaced then the one with the shortest travel distance form the 1st point is chosen.

Input:

Point # Latitude Longitude Azimuth
1
2

Latitude Longitude

Distance from initial points to the ortodrome crosspoint:
